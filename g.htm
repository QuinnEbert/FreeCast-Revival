How to Broadcast

You will need:
Winamp 5.03
ShoutCast DSP Plugin


Once Winamp and the ShoutCast DSP Plugin are installed
Open Winamp
In Winamp push Ctrl+P
A 'Winamp Preferences' box should open up
From the right menu in this box choose 'DSP/Effect' under 'Plug-Ins'


On the right you should now see a number of options including 'Nullsoft SHOUTcast Source DSP v1.8.2b
Highlit this and a 'SHOUTcast Source' screen should open.
Click the 'Encoder' tab

Here you should configure the quality of your stream
Leave 'Encoder' as 'Encoder 1' and 'Encoder Type' and 'MP3 Encoder'
The encoder settings are largely up to you, here is a guide to help you make a decision as to what you require:
quality reccomended kbps
voice 24 - 64
music 64 - 128

internet connection reccomended kbps
dialup Below 56kbps
broadband Below 128kbps


please try to avoid using anything greater than 192kbps, as this can be very costly on our bandwidth.

Once you have finished configuring 'Encoder' click the 'Output' tab.
Fill in the address as 'bobbyautodj.tbrnhost.com'
Then enter your password and push Connect!


Then load your MP3s into Winamp and push Play,
You're now streaming live on Free Cast Radio!
